    Teams

    Rules

    Format

    Teams compete in a team vs. team water battle bracket. Each week, your team is assigned another team to eliminate.

    Eliminations

    Each team's goal is to eliminate members of the opposing team. Once a player is eliminated, they are out for the remainder of that match but may assist teammates. Eliminated players may not shoot the opposing team.

    Scoring

    • +1 point per kill
    • +1 point per player remaining on your team

    Bracket Progression

    The winning team advances in the Winners Bracket. The losing team moves to the Losers Bracket. If a team loses twice, they are eliminated from the tournament.

    Winning

    Matches continue through both brackets until one final team remains undefeated or wins the final match.
